what he called poesie or poetry in paint Here Venus tries to
stop her lover from departing for the hunt
fearing—correctly—that he would be killed The mood of sen-
suality conveyed by the beautiful depiction of Venus seen
from the back enhances the viewer’s sense of the tragic end

the frightened Cupid Titian’s workshop made multiple
