Italian Renaissance - subjects with which he acquired close
familiarity during a stay in Italy from 1600 to 1608 The

Renaissance and antique works In his treatise De Imitatione

sculpture He also amassed an important collection of antique
portrait busts Among these is a celebrated Roman portrait
then believed to represent the Stoic philosopher Seneca

drawings This sheet drawn after the Seneca bust was
preparatory for a series of engravings depicting portraits
