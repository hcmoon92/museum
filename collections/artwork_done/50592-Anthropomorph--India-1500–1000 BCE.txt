"These anthropomorphic figures harpoons ax blades celts and
antennae swords were cast and hammered from unalloyed copper
They may be dated to 1500 to 1000 BC Given that pure copper
is a relatively soft metal and most of the objects show
little or no signs of wear it seems likely that their
function was largely dedicatory Hoards of such objects have
been found across north India the greatest concentration
being in Uttar Pradesh The findspots suggest they were
