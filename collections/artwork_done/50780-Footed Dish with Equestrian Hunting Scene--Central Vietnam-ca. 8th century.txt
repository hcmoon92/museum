"This remarkable bronze presentation dish can be attributed
to the Cham territories of Vietnam It has an elaborately
decorated cavetto with three equestrian riders wielding a

forested landscape populated by elephants buffalo and lions
It probably served as a presentation dish in a court No
precedent for this object was known until the recent
discovery of a second dish in a hoard excavated in Lam Dong
Province southern Vietnam Both dishes are likely the
products of court workshops associated with a Cham kingdom
in the late first millennium
