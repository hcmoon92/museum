metalwork particularly those with ritual or ceremonial
functions The shape of this piece is based on an archaic
tripod vessel known as a ding which was used for holding or
serving food and other offerings however this object
functioned as an incense burner The six-character mark on
the raised gilt-bronze plaque identifies that it was from
