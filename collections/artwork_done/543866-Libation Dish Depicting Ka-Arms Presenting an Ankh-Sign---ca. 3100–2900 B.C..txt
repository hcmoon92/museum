shape of two intricately linked hieroglyphs The two bent
arms that frame three sides of the dish are read ka the word

ankh meaning life or to live The combination could be
interpreted as the phrase life to thy spirit or as the name
of a person Ankh-ka The dish was undoubtedly used to pour a
purifying liquid probably water that would take on the
