degree of confidence for a nine-year-old girl even one who

before deciding to show Mäda standing tall in a specially-
made dress amid a profusion of springlike patterns The
picture testifies to the sophisticated taste of her parents
banker and industrialist Otto Primavesi and his wife Eugenia
