"Qian Weicheng was one of the foremost Orthodox School
landscapists and flower painters of the eighteenth century
Enjoying a brilliant official career Qian rose to the rank
of vice-president of the Board of Works At the same time the
Qianlong emperor considered him to be one of the outstanding
painters of the age In over twenty years' service at court
Qian Weicheng produced more than 275 paintings for the
emperor Even after Qian's death in 1772 the emperor
continued as on this album to add appreciative inscriptions
This album originally formed part of a four-volume set
depicting scenery and flowers of the four seasons
Representing the varied faces of winter the twelve paintings
in the album alternate between the soft grays and muted
colors of wintry landscapes and the intense colors of

1772년 천위청이 사망한 후에도 건륭제는 이 앨범처럼

그림들은 겨울의 차가운 공기와 고요함을 생생하게 묘사합니다
