"Shoes-Vincent van Gogh-Dutch-1888",
"Van Gogh painted several still lifes of shoes or boots
during his Paris period This picture painted later in Arles
evinces a unique return to the earlier motif However here
Van Gogh has placed the shoes within a specific spatial
context namely the red-tile floor of the Yellow House Not
only may we identify the setting but perhaps the owner of
the shoes as well It has been suggested that this still life
of old peasants' shoes may have been those of Patience
Escalier whose portrait Van Gogh executed around the same
time late summer 1888",
