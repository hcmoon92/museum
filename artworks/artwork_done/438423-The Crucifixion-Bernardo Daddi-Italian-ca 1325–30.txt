Although damaged in part this Crucifixion is an important
early work attributed to Bernardo Daddi one of the principal
painters of Florence Dating perhaps to about 1320 it may
have formed one wing of a diptych for private devotion The
poses of the two seated figures are based on Roman
sculptural prototypes and are particularly beautiful Their
solidly conceived forms and grave expressions are a
testament to the example of Giotto",
