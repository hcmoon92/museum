These illusionistic depictions of allegorical sculptures
from the Palazzo Valle Marchesini Sala in Vicenza were part
of an immersive fresco cycle completed around 1760 and
detached around 1900 Incised lines still visible in raking
light helped to guide Tiepolo as he painted quickly before
the walls’ wet plaster surface dried Tiepolo consulted a
specialist in perspective Mengozzi to ensure that the
foreshortened depictions of architecture and sculpture
aligned when one stood at the center of the room evidence of
just how site-specific such decorative programs could be",
